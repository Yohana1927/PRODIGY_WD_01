ABOUT US


Dunkin' Donuts, founded in 1950 by William Rosenberg in Quincy, Massachusetts, has grown into a global icon renowned for its coffee and donuts. Originally named "Open Kettle," the brand quickly evolved under the Dunkin' Donuts name, pioneering the concept of serving high-quality coffee and freshly made donuts in a fast and affordable manner. Over the decades, Dunkin' has expanded its menu to include a diverse array of baked goods, sandwiches, and beverages, all centered around its core offerings of coffee and donuts.

At the heart of Dunkin' Donuts' success is its commitment to quality and innovation. The brand sources premium Arabica beans for its coffee, ensuring a consistently rich and smooth taste across its extensive range of hot and iced beverages.
